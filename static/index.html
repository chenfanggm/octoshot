Octoshot

A 3d first person shooter for the web!

Rules

  • Games can have 2-8 players
  • Only headshots count -- but you have big heads!
  • Everyone has 3 points of health (3 shots kills somebody)
  • Use WASD or the arrow keys to move around
  • If in fullscreen mode, shoot with a mouse click and look around with the mouse
  • If in windowed mode, shoot with the E key and click and drag to look around
  • Get the most kills in 5 minutes!

Instructions

Use WASD or arrow keys to move around, and space to jump.

In fullscreen mode, use mouse to look around and click shoot.

In windowed mode, click and drag to look around and hit E to shoot.
